Contact Email: iqstackltd@gmail.com Location: California, USA Intended Age Group: 5–12 years old IQ Stack Ltd. (“we,” “our,” “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of all players, especially children. Block Builder (“the Game”) is designed for children ages 5–12 and complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families Program requirements. This Privacy Policy explains how the Game handles information and what data is or is not collected. 1. Information We Collect We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of the Game. The Game does not request or access: Names Email addresses Location services Photos, camera, or microphone Contact lists Device files Account creation or login Purchases or payment information There is no user registration, and no personal data ever reaches our servers. 2. Third-Party Services (AdMob Only) Block Builder uses Google AdMob to display interstitial ads. AdMob is configured with the following child-safe settings: AdMob.initialize({ requestTrackingAuthorization: false, initializeForTesting: false, tagForChildDirectedTreatment: true, maxAdContentRating: 'G' }); This ensures: G-rated ads only No personalized ads No tracking for ad personalization No interest-based advertising COPPA-compliant ad delivery AdMob may automatically collect non-personal anonymous data, such as: Device type Non-precise location (approximate region) Ad interactions Crash or performance data This information is used only by Google to deliver contextual ads and prevent fraud. We do not access, store, or use this information. You can review Google’s privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy 3. In-App Review API The Game includes the official Google Play In-App Review API, which allows users to rate the app without leaving the game. No personal data is collected. We do not see or store any information related to the review flow. The API is fully operated and controlled by Google. There are no external links, social features, or access to websites. 4. Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance) Because Block Builder is designed for children: We do not collect personal information We do not allow user-generated content We do not include chat or social sharing We do not allow personalized advertising We use only G-rated, child-safe, contextual ads We do not provide external links that children could open If a parent or guardian believes that personal data may have been accidentally submitted, they can contact us at iqstackltd@gmail.com, and we will address the request immediately—though the Game is not designed to collect any such data. 5. International Users Block Builder is available worldwide. We do not collect or process personal data, but Google AdMob may process anonymous ad delivery data under its own global privacy standards, including: GDPR (Europe) CCPA (California) LGPD (Brazil) Since we do not collect personal information, no international data transfer is performed by us. 6. Security Because we do not collect or retain personal data, there is no personal information at risk. Google is responsible for securing any anonymous data collected by AdMob. 7. Your Rights Since no personal data is collected: There is no data to delete No account to deactivate No profile to access or change No opt-outs are required Users may disable ads personalization at the device level, though the Game already uses non-personalized ads only. 8.
